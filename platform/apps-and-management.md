# Apps And Management

Hosted apps and management are coming soon.

When the hosted platform launches, this page will cover:

- app creation
- routes, actions, and behavior configuration
- voice and instruction settings
- testing and debugging in the hosted control plane
- how hosted app configuration connects to `appId` usage in the client SDK
